# …fan mail

We'd like to invite artists that want to perform/exibit at Doom-It-Yourself events to send us a request via mail.
Visual artists can write to us with enclosed: a short description of your work or your ideas for projects with us and some display of your art.
Musicians should send us a request and a way to listen to your music, a cassette, bandcamp or soundcloud link works best.
→submit@doomityourself.org
