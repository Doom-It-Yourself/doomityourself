# Introduction

Welcome to the DoomIY Wiki Guide, an online collection of knowledge and know-how compiled by both our own staff and those in our community. The articles that currently reside in this wiki are the result of an organic free volunteer effort and we invite you to participate as well. Improve existing articles or create new ones or just browse the information to help you along with your own project. Read the Dev Guidelines for some hints about how to submit new pages.
