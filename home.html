# You've reached the website of Doom-It-Yourself

A platform for anti-commercial culture that tries to Keep It Simple.

Currently we try to provide everything a poor man needs during war; from cultural events to recipes for healthy food and homemade machinery.

We hope to build a community that is diverse and helpful, so we can pride ourselves on a range of skillsets and uses for DoomIY that stem from it.
Please check out our forums and mailing list to get your hands dirty. Also glance through our wiki if you want to learn more about DoomIY.

# A new year: time for a sollution!

Dear fellowship of freak brothers,

The problem with music these days is the commercial shit, right?
The goddamn head-up-yr-ass cheesy weak brainless pathetic middle-class high-school glamour meth whores. And when you try to put some loud music on to kill the pain brought upon you by this shallow society the police come bashing in and...
Euhm, wait...

We actually wanted to make the following announcement:
We are very thrilled to introduce you to a new platform against commerce, against endorphin!

Are you also fed up with the lack of anything that can accompany you on a murderous strange day? Then this might be something for you!
This is an attempt to do something different and meaningful; a platform for anti-commercial culture. Powered by napalm and muddy drugfreak volunteers.
That in itself might not be that innovative, but it is an experiment with the implentation of DIY ideas and organizational methods onto new genres and artforms in the hope that this will inspire and create new platforms for non-commercial culture like HDDT did for us.
What we think is innovative of this platform is the approach to involve Open Source and its community to this way of organizing. So that everybody can learn and contribute change.

Dark times make possibilities so if you also think it is important to give more attention to subcultures, come together and make our mission possible.
Hugs, kisses and broken fingers,

The Doom-It-Yourself HQ
